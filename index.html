my biography

Hello! Welcome to this blog! my name is Lian Khosravi. I'm 19 and currently a CE student in Isfahan University and I'm honored to share my favorite projects and pieces of art with you. I hope you enjoy!

My Projects

Text Machine: One my latest projects. This machine takes a text and work on that texts as you wish! For instance, it can delete part of that text, add more sentences to that, find your wanted word on that and even swap words and letters with each other!

Lottery File: It's an excel file that has a list of names and can choose one person from all names and announce them as the winner of our lottery! It's totally random and each time it'll give a different name!

MineSweeper: We are all aquainted with this nostalgic game that we had to choose the right square that did not contain a bomb in it. well, one of my final project was this fun game!

LMC: Little Man Computer! I have made a bunch of fun functions in this framework. Power function, arrangement function, absolute function and so on
